## Chapter 1 – The Relic’s Guard

Their swords clang, echoing the clash of ideals that fuels the air. Aric is assigned to guard the ancient citadel’s artifact.

In a brief moment of calm, their eyes meet and linger longer than expected. Lira arrives seeking the same relic for her research.

Heat rises as the stone walls seem to pulse with their unresolved anger. A storm forces them inside together.

"We’re forced together, Aric," Lira whispered.
